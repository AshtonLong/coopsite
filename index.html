Government operations are often perceived as slow and manual. My objective was to shatter that perception.

During the Summer 2025 term with the Ontario Public Service (OPS), specifically within the Ministry of Public & Business Services & Procurement, I transitioned from a student to a critical engineer. Based in Guelph, I was tasked with modernizing the "nervous system" of the province's digital infrastructure.

The mission was clear: Eliminate toil. enhance reliability, and deploy intelligent automation.
